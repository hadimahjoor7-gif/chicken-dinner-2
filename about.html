Meet the Owner

Abid Khan — Founder & Head Cook

Owner-operator, Chicken Dinner · Rahmoo, Pulwama

Abid Khan founded Chicken Dinner as a small family kitchen with one goal: serve tandoor-fresh chicken the way it's meant to be eaten — hot off the fire, never reheated, never rushed. Today he still runs the tandoor himself most nights.

Known locally for his hands-on approach, Abid personally oversees every order that leaves the kitchen, from marination to plating, and has built the business into one of Rahmoo's most trusted names for a proper chicken dinner.
